Pandit Deendayal Energy University

Each Hostel of the University offers a variety of Social and Academic oriented activities that make it possible to learn more not only about you but also about those living in hostel. Our in-campus residential facilities offer furnished hostel rooms on three seat sharing basis subject to its availability. This is an optional facility offered to the students. Separate hostels are for female students. There are Wi-Fi enabled hostel blocks which are well-designed to meet general needs of the students. These include emergency medical facilities, a doctor on call and a well equipped and comfortable lounge to facilitate group activities.
